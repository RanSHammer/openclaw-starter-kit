# GOALS.md — Goals & Objectives Tracker

Track quarterly goals here. Your agent checks this during heartbeats and surfaces approaching deadlines.

## How to Use

- Add goals under the current quarter
- Each goal has a status, due date, and progress notes
- Your agent will flag goals with approaching deadlines or no recent progress
- Update status as things move: `[ ]` → `[~]` → `[x]`

**Status legend:**
- `[ ]` — Not started
- `[~]` — In progress
- `[x]` — Done
- `[-]` — Dropped / deprioritized
